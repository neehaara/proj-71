Vaimitra Chandrasehar publishes her new book!

Young Author

We are proud and happy to announce that Vaimitra Chandrasehar, young author and pride of our school, has published her fourth book titled "The Great adventures of Ally and Albert- part3- When life is an adventure, literally " . This is the third book in the same series . The launch was done virtually on 30th May,2021 by the Skillshark edutech team and was presided over by Chief guests from the field of literature. Vaimitra published her first book "Take a break" at the age of seven making her the youngest author in Chennai. The plot, choice of words ,Clarity in her thought, and attention to detail make her books a very interesting read. The management, Staff and students wish her the very best for all her future endeavors !
